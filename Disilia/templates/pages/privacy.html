01. Introduction

At Disilia House, we believe that privacy is not merely a legal requirement; it is a fundamental pillar of luxury. Your trust is our most valuable asset. This Privacy Manifesto outlines how we protect and respect your digital identity as you interact with our curated ecosystem.

This policy applies to all platforms operated under the Disilia brand, ensuring that our commitment to excellence extends from the physical products we curate to the data safeguards we implement.

02. Data Sovereignty

We maintain the principle of Identity Sovereignty. You remain the sole owner of your personal information. We operate as custodians, ensuring that your data remains secure, uncompromised, and used exclusively to enhance your Disilia experience.

03. Collected Data Nodes

To provide a bespoke commerce experience, we collect specific data points during your journey:

  • A. Identity Attributes: Full name, executive address for logistics, and authenticated digital contact methods.
  • B. Transaction Logs: Historical curation preferences, acquisition history, and preferred settlement methods.
  • C. Digital Footprints: Interaction patterns on our 3D interfaces and category-specific interest nodes.

04. Strategic Usage

The processing of your data is governed by strict utility guidelines:

  • Bespoke Personalization: Adapting our catalog to show the products most relevant to your lifestyle.
  • Secure Logistics: Ensuring your orders are delivered to your precise location with absolute reliability.
  • Identity Protection: Using advanced analytics to prevent unauthorized access to your account.

05. Global Portability

Our infrastructure is built for the global vision. We adhere to international data regulations, including the GDPR (EU) and CCPA (US), ensuring that your rights are universal regardless of your geographical location.

06. Security Protocols

Disilia employs military-grade encryption for all sensitive data transfers. Our servers are housed in Tier-4 data centers, monitored 24/7 by our security executive team. We conduct regular external audits to ensure our 'Zero-Breach' standard is maintained.

07. Executive Rights

Under our manifesto, you possess the right to:

  • Request a complete transcript of your data nodes.
  • Rectify any inaccuracies in your identity file.
  • Invoke the 'Right to Oblivion' (Complete deletion of your digital profile).
